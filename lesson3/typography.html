Heading Fonts

Staatliches

I chose Staatliches as a heading font for site name and large headlines. I chose the font for its style, not necessarily for readability. The font will be used for short headlines like the site and page names.

Patua One

I chose Patua One as a secondary heading font, with less emphasis on style and more on readability. The font will be used for longer headlines like section headers and navigation.

And behold, there were many of the Nephites who did enter therein and did sail forth with much provisions, and also many women and children; and they took their course northward. And thus ended the thirty and seventh year.

I chose Lato as a primary paragraph font because it is clean and very readable, and has a good contrast with the heading fonts.
