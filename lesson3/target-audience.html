The Target Audeince

The target audience are adult residents and frequent visitors to Stormwatch Bay. They engage in work or leisure activities on the water or along the coast. They own or rent boats.

Personas

Persona #1: John


Photo by Kampus Production from Pexels

John is a Stormwatch Bay native and has lived around the Bay his whole life. He is thirty-three years old. He owns and manages a small store in downtown Fish Haven. He is in the lower middle class income bracket. He is married and has two children in grade school. He usually browses websites using a smartphone.

Persona #2: Red


Photo by Lara Jameson from Pexels

Red is forty-seven years old. He lives and works in a large city that is a two-hour drive from Stormwatch Bay. He is single, with no children. He is a lawyer earning an upper-middle class income. He owns a boat that he docks at one of the marinas around Stormwatch Bay. He is a very avid fisher and drives up to the Bay for a weekend fishing trip about twenty times a year.

Scenarios

  • John hears that a hurricane is approaching the coast. He wants to know when to expect the storm and what preparations he needs to make.
  • Red is planning a fishing trip with several friends. He wants to know if the weather conditions next weekend are good for fishing.
  • John is thinking of painting his store. He wants to know if rain is expected this week.
  • Red is thinking of renting an apartment in Soda Springs. He wants to know if an apartment he is looking at gets hit hard by hurricanes.
  • John and his wife are planning to celebrate their anniversary by renting a yacht and having a celebration on the water. John wants to know if the weather on that day is going to be smooth.
