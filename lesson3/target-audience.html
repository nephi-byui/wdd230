Personas

John

John is a Stormwatch Bay native and has lived around the Bay his whole life. He is forty-eight years old. He owns and manages a small hardware store in downtown Fish Haven. He is in the lower middle class income bracket. He is married and has three children, one of them in high school and two of them in grade school. He usually browses websites using a smartphone.

Red

Red is thirty-seven years old. He lives and works in a large city that is a two-hour drive from Stormwatch Bay. He is a lawyer earning an upper-middle class income. He owns a boat that he docks at one of the marinas around Stormwatch Bay. He is a very avid fisher and drives up to the Bay for a weekend fishing trip about twenty times a year.
