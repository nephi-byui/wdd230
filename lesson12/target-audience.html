The Target Audience

The target audience are people who are financially invested in Quezon City: business owners, employees, both resident and non-resident.

Personas

Juan


Juan is a small business owner from the Second District. He is forty-three years old. He manages a business with twenty-five employees. He does not consider himself politically active but likes to keep abreast of events that affect him and his business.

Jose


Jose is twenty-seven years old. He lives and works in Quezon City. He commutes to work every day. He has a wife and three young children.

Pedro


Pedro is a lives and works in a town adjacent to Quezon City. He owns a business that employs twenty people. He is fifty-two years old, married and has five children and thirteen grandchildren.

Scenarios

  • Juan learns that the government is planning to implement pandemic-related restrictions, with certain businesses not being allowed to operate. He wants to know whether his business is affected.
  • Jose is unhappy in the field he is working in, and wants to explore other industries. He wants to know what if any other large employers in Quezon City are hiring, and what fields they are expanding in.
  • Juan just finished renewing his business permits for the year, and he is exhausted from all the red tape he had to wade through. He wants to know whether the Chamber of Commerce is doing anything to lobby towards eliminating red tape.
  • Jose is thinking of leaving employment and starting his own business. He wants to know how to become a member of the Quezon City Chamber of Commerce.
  • Pedro is a business owner in a neighboring city. He is thinking of expanding his operations with a Quezon City branch. He wants to make partnerships that can help him in his expansion.
