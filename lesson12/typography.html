Heading Fonts

FORUM

I chose Forum as a font for the top headers because it exudes an air of respectability and gravitas that seems to fit the organization that the website will represent. This font will be used for the site name and some headlines. I will use this font with all-uppercase characters only.

Merriweather Sans 500

I chose Merriweather Sans 500 as a secondary heading font, with less emphasis on style and more on readability. The font will be used for longer headlines like section headers and navigation.
