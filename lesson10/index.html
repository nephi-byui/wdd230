Stormwatch Bay

The beautiful towns of Stormwatch Bay, located on the western coast of Idaho, look out across the Oregon and Washington Seas, and are a popular destination for vacationers, fishing enthusiasts, retirees, and island hoppers from the Nevada island chains to the south. Stormwatch Tower provides weather news for the towns of Stormwatch Bay, making sure you always know if a hurricane is coming from across the Pacific, and how to schedule your fishing trip around it.
