Stormwatch Bay

The beautiful towns of Stormwatch Bay, located on the western coast of Idaho, look out across the Oregon and Washington Seas, and are a popular destination for vacationers, fishing enthusiasts, retirees, and island hoppers from the Nevada island chains to the south. Stormwatch Tower provides weather news for the towns of Stormwatch Bay, making sure you always know if a hurricane is coming from across the Pacific, and how to schedule your fishing trip around it.

The coastal state of Idaho has always been a great destination for people who love water. Overlooking the great Pacific Ocean, there is no doubt that Idaho is a great place to live. People who love fishing can come to Stormwatch Bay and go fishing.

Avid fishers come from around the state to fish. Stormwatch Bay's towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven are dotted with marinas, beaches, and waterfront destinations.

In real life, Idaho is a landlocked state, bordered to the west by Washington and Oregon.
